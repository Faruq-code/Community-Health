About Us

The Community Health Feedback & Alert System is designed to empower communities by providing a platform to report public health issues, share alerts, and promote awareness for a safer and healthier environment.

Our Mission

To create a reliable, anonymous, and accessible reporting system that allows individuals to flag health hazards and receive timely alerts, ensuring swift action and collective safety.

Why It Matters

Early detection and reporting of public health threats can save lives, improve community well-being, and help authorities respond effectively.
